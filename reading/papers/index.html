      # Compression

      • Compact 3D Gaussian Representation for Radiance Field. [Paper] (opens new window) [Notes]
      • Compact3D: Compressing Gaussian Splat Radiance Field Models with Vector Quantization. [Paper] (opens new window)
        • kkk-means codebook (quantize color, SH, scale, and rotation parameters separately, resulting in 4 codebooks, do not quantize opacity)
        • order of Gaussians doesn't matter, sort and do run-length encoding
      • EAGLES: Efficient Accelerated 3D Gaussians with Lightweight EncodingS. [Paper] (opens new window)
        • quantize color, rotation and opacity (do not quantize SH, scaling, position)
      • Compact 3D Scene Representation via Self-Organizing Gaussian Grids. [Paper] (opens new window)
        • reduction factor of 8x to 26x in size

      # 4DGS

      • Dynamic 3D Gaussians: Tracking by Persistent Dynamic View Synthesis. [Paper] (opens new window)

      Newer

      • An Efficient 3D Gaussian Representation for Monocular/Multi-view Dynamic Scenes. [Paper] (opens new window)
        • reprensent Guassian positions and rotations as functions of time (Fourier approximation), two-stage optimization(?)
        • flow loss (RAFT as GT)
        • follow 3DGS on densification and pruning(?)
      • 4D Gaussian Splatting for Real-Time Dynamic Scene Rendering. [Paper] (opens new window)
        • 82 FPS at an 800×800 resolution on RTX 3090, 30 FPS at a resolution of 1352×1014
        • represent Gaussian motions and shape changes by an efficient Gaussian deformation field network (kkk-planes)
      • Real-time Photorealistic Dynamic Scene Representation and Rendering with 4D Gaussian Splatting. [Paper] (opens new window)
        • 4D Gaussians(?)
      • MD-Splatting: Learning Metric Deformation from 4D Gaussians in Highly Deformable Scenes. [Paper] (opens new window)
      • DynMF: Neural Motion Factorization for Real-time Dynamic View Synthesis with 3D Gaussian Splatting. [Paper] (opens new window)
      • Gaussian-Flow: 4D Reconstruction with Dynamic 3D Gaussian Particle. [Paper] (opens new window)
        • formulate a 4D scene as a set of deformable 3D Gaussian points
        • A novel Dual-Domain Deformation Model (DDDM) is proposed to explicitly model deformations of each Gaussian point's attributes
          • The time-dependent deformation residual is modeled simultaneously in time and frequency domains (polynomials + Fourier series)
          • models position, rotation and radiance; scaling and opacity remain constant
        • Moreover, an adaptive timestamp scaling technique(?) is introduced to avoid overfitting the scene to only frames with violent motions
        • For robust estimation, we also regularize the motion trajectory by
          • the KNN-based rigid regularization
          • the time smooth constraints
        • two stage optimization (with and without Gaussian densification/pruning)
      • HiFi4G: High-Fidelity Human Performance Rendering via Compact Gaussian Splatting. [Paper] (opens new window) [Notes]
      • SWAGS: Sampling Windows Adaptively for Dynamic 3D Gaussian Splatting. [Paper] (opens new window)
        • canonical space + deformation field
        • adaptively partition a dynamic sequence into windows based on 2D optical flow
